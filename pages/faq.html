FAQs

How do I make a reservation?

See more

You can make a reservation through our website by selecting your desired car type, pickup and return dates, and any additional extras you may need. Alternatively, you can also contact our customer service team for assistance.

What documents do I need to bring for pickup?

See more

You will need to bring a valid driver's license, a credit card for payment and deposit, and any other documents specified in your reservation confirmation email.

What is included in the rental price?

See more

The rental price typically includes basic insurance coverage and unlimited mileage. Additional extras such as GPS, child seats, and insurance upgrades may be available for an extra charge.

What is the minimum age requirement to rent a car?

See more

The minimum age requirement varies depending on the location and car rental company. In most cases, the minimum age is 21 years old, but additional fees or restrictions may apply for drivers under 25.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

See more

Yes, we offer long-term rental options for customers who need a vehicle for an extended period. Our long-term rental packages typically provide discounted rates for rentals lasting a week or more. Whether you need a car for a month-long vacation or a several-month business trip, we can accommodate your needs. Contact our customer service team to discuss your specific requirements and get a quote for a long-term rental.
